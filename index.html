Our List Of Solutions and Services

We help clients get thier vehicles registered with ease from the comfort of their home.
By submitting the necessary documents to us we process through the appropriate bodies
and monitor it to ensure timely delivery.

Vehicle Registrations (New or Toks)

This include new number plate, proof of ownership certificate, road worthiness, central motor registry reciept, vehicle license, insurance, computerized vehicle inspection, number tag, reflective tape and autoVIN certificate.

Renewal of Vehicle Documents

We provide renewal services of the following documents: Vehicle license, Road worthiness certificate and insurance.

Driver's License Processing

We offer fast driver's license applications both for fresh applicants and renewal with 3 or 5 years validity.

Customized number plates, Dealer number plates

We offer processing of customized number plates for vehicle owners along with dealer plates for vehicle dealers and genuine tint permits.
